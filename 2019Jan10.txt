Questions for interview:
https://youtu.be/Y95eI-ek_E8

Connect
1. How did you come to work here?
2. What do you love most about working here?

Culture 
3. Tell me about the most successful hire you made recently?
    What did they do that made them successful in their role?
4. Tell me about a hire who failed recently. 
    Why weren't they successful in their role?

    NB: if the successful candidate worked 80 hours a week for
        no pay, you probably don't want to work there!

Challenges
5. What's the company's biggest challenge this year
    and how will this job overcome it?
6. How will I measure my performance so I know I am having
    a positive impact on this challenge?

Close
7. What additional experience do you wish I had that
    would make me a better fit for the job?
    
    -question 7 helps you understand and address any
        reservations they might have about hiring you.

8. What are the next steps in the process?
    - gives you a sense for whether you passed
