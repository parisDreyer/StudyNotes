- to disable comments for a post -- in the site editor go to settings:Discussion:<bool>AllowCommentsForFuturePosts
and adjust AllowCommentsForFuturePosts to false
The next article you post will have comments disabled. Then you can set it back to true to re-enable comments for subsequent posts.
